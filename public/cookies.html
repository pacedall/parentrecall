1. About this Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how ParentRecall uses cookies, local storage, app storage and similar technologies.

ParentRecall is designed to avoid advertising cookies and unnecessary tracking. The technologies described here are mainly used to keep the service secure, keep users signed in and provide core functionality.

2. What cookies and local storage are

Cookies are small files stored on your device by a website. Local storage and app storage are similar technologies that allow information to be stored on your browser or device.

In this policy, “cookies” includes cookies, local storage, session storage, app storage, software development kit storage and similar technologies where appropriate.

3. Essential cookies and storage

ParentRecall uses essential cookies and storage for purposes such as:

  • keeping you signed in;
  • remembering session status;
  • protecting account security;
  • supporting “keep me signed in” functionality where selected;
  • preventing abuse and unauthorised access;
  • making the website and app work correctly.

These are necessary for the service. If you block them, ParentRecall may not function properly.

4. Analytics and advertising cookies

ParentRecall does not currently use advertising cookies or third-party advertising trackers.

ParentRecall does not use cookies to sell personal data, build advertising profiles or show targeted adverts.

If privacy-friendly analytics are introduced in the future, we will update this policy. If analytics or other non-essential cookies require consent, we will ask for consent before using them.

5. App Store and mobile app technologies

If you use ParentRecall through Apple App Store or Google Play, Apple, Google and your device operating system may process certain technical information under their own privacy and platform terms.

ParentRecall may also use app storage to keep you signed in and support app functionality. This is treated in the same way as essential website storage.

6. Managing cookies

You can usually manage or block cookies through your browser settings. You can also delete local storage through your browser or device settings.

Blocking essential cookies or storage may prevent you from logging in or using ParentRecall properly.

7. Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if our use of cookies, storage or similar technologies changes.

Where required, we will request consent before using non-essential cookies.

8. Contact

Questions about cookies or storage should be sent to team@parentrecall.com.
