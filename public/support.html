Contacting us

For anything at all — a question, a problem, a feature idea, a privacy request, or to report something — email team@parentrecall.com. To help us help you quickly, please tell us the email address on your account and, if something isn’t working, the device and browser you’re using and what you expected to happen.

Common questions

How much does ParentRecall cost?

ParentRecall is free to register and use. There is no card required and no catch.

I’ve forgotten my password.

On the sign-in screen, choose Forgot password and enter your email. We’ll send a reset link that’s valid for one hour. For your security, the link can only be used once.

How do I install the app on my phone?

ParentRecall is a web app, so there’s nothing to download from an app store to get started. Open parentrecall.com in your phone’s browser and choose Add to Home Screen (Safari on iPhone) or Install app (Chrome on Android).

How do I add my partner?

The account admin can invite a partner by email from the account menu. You’ll then share the same private lists, so you’re both covered.

Why am I only signed in on two devices?

For security, ParentRecall keeps you signed in on up to two devices at once. Signing in on a third device signs out the oldest one automatically.

Can I get my data out?

Yes. From your account you can export your lists, either as a spreadsheet or as a data file, at any time.

Your account

You can update your details, invite or remove a partner, export your data, and delete your account from the account menu inside the app. Deleting your account is permanent and removes your data — you can also do this at parentrecall.com/delete-account.

Privacy & your data

Your lists are private to your household and any partner you invite. There are no public profiles, no social feed, and no photographs of children — faces are friendly cartoon avatars only. To understand exactly what we store and why, see our Privacy Policy. To make a data access, correction or deletion request, email team@parentrecall.com.

Reporting a problem or abuse

If something looks wrong or is being misused, please tell us. You can report abuse from inside the app using the Report abuse option, or email team@parentrecall.com. For anything involving the safety of a child, please see our Child Safety Standards, which explain how to report a concern and how we respond.
