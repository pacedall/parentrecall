2. What ParentRecall is

ParentRecall is a private memory aid for parents, guardians and carers. It helps adults remember names and basic details of people connected with their child’s school, clubs, classes and activities, such as classmates, fellow parents, coaches and instructors.

ParentRecall is deliberately not a social network. There are no public profiles, feeds, comments, messaging tools, friend-finding features, public directories or ways for one household to search for another household’s data.

The service is designed around data minimisation. It does not allow photo uploads, does not store photographs, and uses illustrated avatar settings rather than real images.

You may only use ParentRecall where you have a genuine personal or household reason to remember the information you enter. For example, using it to remember your child’s classmates, other parents, teachers, coaches or club contacts is acceptable. Using it to build lists about people for unrelated, commercial, intrusive or harmful purposes is not acceptable.

ParentRecall is not intended for children to create accounts or use directly.

5. Household accounts and partner access

ParentRecall may allow a household account to invite one partner, spouse, co-parent, guardian or carer to share the household data.

The account owner or household administrator controls the account. The administrator may add or remove children, clubs, classes and people, invite or remove the partner, export the household data and delete the account.

An invited partner may view, add and edit information within the household account, but may have more limited permissions than the administrator.

If you invite another person, you confirm that you have their permission to invite them and that they are an appropriate person to access the household account. Removing a partner will revoke their access, but information they previously added may remain unless the administrator edits or deletes it.

You must not enter unnecessary sensitive information, including health, medical, disability, religion, ethnicity, political opinion, sexual orientation or other special-category information. ParentRecall is a name-and-face memory aid and does not need that information.

7. Privacy-by-design controls

ParentRecall includes controls intended to reduce privacy risk. These include:

  • children are recorded by first name only;
  • surnames of people added to the service are automatically shortened to three characters;
  • parent/carer names are limited in length;
  • no photographs or uploaded images are stored;
  • avatars are illustrated configurations selected by the user;
  • household lists are private and not searchable by others;
  • there is no public directory, feed or social graph; and
  • users can export or delete their account data.

You must not attempt to bypass, disable, manipulate or work around these safeguards.
